AI Data Processing & Third-Party Integration

To provide features such as automated receipt scanning, voice transaction entry, and financial insights, our Service integrates with third-party Artificial Intelligence (AI) providers, specifically Google Gemini API and DeepSeek API.

Detailed Disclosure of AI Data Handling:
  • Data Shared: When you use AI features (Scan Receipt, Voice Input, or Chat), we transmit specific data to our AI providers. This includes images of receipts, audio recordings/transcriptions of your financial inputs, and transaction notes.
  • Third-Party Providers: Data is processed by Google (Gemini API) and DeepSeek (DeepSeek API). These providers act as data processors and are chosen for their high standards of data protection.
  • Purpose of Processing: Data is shared solely to perform real-time extraction (converting images/voice to structured text) and to provide personalized financial insights.
  • No PII Exposure: We do NOT share your name, email address, phone number, or any account credentials with these AI services. We only send the raw transactional content required for analysis.
  • Data Protection & Training: We utilize enterprise-grade API versions. In accordance with the providers' terms of service, data submitted via these APIs is not used to train their foundational AI models.
  • User Consent: We obtain your explicit consent within the Application before any data is transmitted to these third-party AI services. You may revoke this consent at any time via the Application settings.
